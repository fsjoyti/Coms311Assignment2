				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p><b>Complexity theory</b> may refer to:</p>
<ul>
<li>It is a study of <a href="/wiki/Complex_system" title="Complex system">complex system</a> or <a href="/wiki/Complex_systems" title="Complex systems">complex systems</a>

<li><a href="/wiki/Complexity_theory_and_organizations" title="Complexity theory and organizations">Complexity theory and organizations</a>, the application of complexity theory to strategy</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Complexity_economics" title="Complexity economics">Complexity economics</a>, the application of complexity theory to economics</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Complex_adaptive_system" title="Complex adaptive system">Complex adaptive system</a>, special case of complex systems</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Chaos_theory" title="Chaos theory">Chaos theory</a>, the study of the behavior of dynamical systems that are highly sensitive to initial conditions</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Computational_complexity_theory" title="Computational complexity theory">Computational complexity theory</a>, a field in theoretical computer science and mathematics</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Algorithmic_information_theory" title="Algorithmic information theory">Algorithmic information theory</a></li>
<li>It is related to chemical kinetics</li>
</ul>
